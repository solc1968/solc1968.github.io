Welcome!

Our purpose is to live our lives for the glory of Jesus so that others can find salvation in Him. As a church, we seek to love all of our neighbors and let them know how much God loves them. It is our prayer that Jesus will transform the communities around us as they discover the truth about God's word and what it means to follow Jesus.
